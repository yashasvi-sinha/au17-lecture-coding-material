Kalam was instrumental in laying the foundation of the rocket launch systems at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) from the late 1960s to 1982.
Dr. Kalam served as the 11th President of India between 2002 and 2007.
Dr. Kalam was honoured with several prestigious awards including India's highest civilian honour Bharat Ratna in 1997.
Dr. Kalam passed away in 2015 while addressing students at IIM, Shillong.
